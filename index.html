Porsche

To the enthusiast: Porsche builds extremely reliable performance cars across a wide enough spectrum of price ranges than any working professional can afford a used one, and can probably stretch the budget to afford a new one. They are easier to drive and less quirky than lower-priced sporty cars like the Miata, the GT86, the 370Z or the pony cars.

To the non-enthusiast: The brand conveys extravagance and luxury. It says "I can afford a car whose only purpose is a toy" (nevermind that they're selling Panameras and SUVs like hotcakes these days, and nevermind that plenty of people daily their Boxsters). The styling is elegant and understated, unlike the cars I mentioned above, and it doesn't really look out of place anywhere.
